                 SONGWRITING
• Facilitates the sharing of feelings, ideas and experiences.
• A means of expressing and understanding fears (ie., Hospitalized
  children.)
• Vehicle for examining feelings about the meaning in life and death
  (ie., Terminal illness)
• Opportunity for creating a legacy or a shared experience with a
  caregiver, child or loved.
