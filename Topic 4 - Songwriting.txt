Song Writing Techniques
●   Brain Storming: thinking about themes and words
●   Fill in the Blanks: using a pre-existing song and changing up
    words
●   Song Parody: changing all the words but melody remains the
    same
●   Song Collage: using pieces of other songs and putting them
    together
●   Rhyming: brainstormed words themes and words
