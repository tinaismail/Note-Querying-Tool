          CBT - MUSIC THERAPY
     Combines cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) with music.
     It uses music to help people change or reinforce behaviors.

Based on the idea that thoughts, feelings, and actions are connected,
      and that negative thoughts can create a negative cycle.

  COMMUNITY MUSIC THERAPY
           • Delve further into this topic later in this course
• Challenges the traditional interventions of clinical music therapy.
• Involves group music therapy techniques designed to improve a common goal within a
  group or to unite a population and provide a sense of community for those who need it.
• People who share common social, societal, or cultural challenges (refugees)
• Patients experiencing a similar illness or medical treatment (Parkinson’s)
• Living in a shared environment, such as a hospital or psychiatric unit, prison ward, long-
  term-care home, or palliative care facility.
