What is Music Therapy?
Music therapy is a discipline in which a credentialed professional (MTA*) uses music purposefully
within a therapeutic relationship to support development, health, and well-being.
Muisc therapists use musci safely and ethically to address human needs within cognitive,
communicative, emotional, musical, physical, social, and spiritual domains,
*Music Therapist Accredited/Musicothérapeute accrédité •
Definition provided by: Canadian Association of Music Therapists (CAMT) – June 2016

IS MUSIC THERAPY NEW?
The health care profession of music therapy has been professionally
regulated in Canada since 1970’s

The concept of music to support health and well being was well established
